Movie Summary

Pariyerum Perumal is a powerful Tamil social drama that follows the life of Pariyan, a young man from a marginalized caste who joins law college with hopes of dignity and justice. His friendship with Jothi, a girl from a dominant caste, provokes violent caste-based hostility, exposing the deep-rooted discrimination and cruelty embedded in society. As Pariyan struggles against humiliation, abuse, and systemic oppression, the film portrays how caste violence affects everyday life and education. Directed by Mari Selvaraj, the movie blends raw realism with emotional depth, highlighting the resilience of the oppressed and delivering a strong message about equality, humanity, and the urgent need for social change.
